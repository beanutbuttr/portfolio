Hey Diary, I think I’m in love
Director/Editor/Writer/Cinematographer

My short film called “Hey Diary, I think I’m in love” is about a journal entry and a love for all kinds of things.

A girl goes to the park to write in her journal about things she loves — simple things like balloons and trees, but also the things she used to love like jumping on the couch and poking her baby sister’s cheeks.

When you meet someone, one of the first things you share are the things you love - your interests, passions, favorite foods and favorite memories. This short film is dedicated to those moments.
